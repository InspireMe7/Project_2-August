    • Rim World Royalty

    The Royalty DLC was the first DLC released for Rim World on the 24.02.2020. It adds a new Faction to the Game called "The Empire" that brings new Game mechanics, Items, Quests and and a new Ending to the Game.

    On February 24, 2020, the first downloadable content (DLC), Royalty, was released with the 1.1 update, adding a new empire faction and psylink technology for colonists to use.
